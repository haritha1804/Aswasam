Transformative Ayurvedic Therapies

Abangya

Experience the soothing power of Abhyanga, a traditional Ayurvedic massage with warm herbal oils. This relaxing treatment improves circulation and balances your body, leaving you refreshed and stress-free.

Read More

Njavarakizhi

Njavarakizhi uses warm bundles of Njavara rice soaked in herbal liquids. It relieves muscle and joint pain, improves circulation, and detoxifies the body, promoting relaxation and reducing stress.

Read More

Pizhichil

Pizhichil is a calming therapy where warm, medicated oil is gently poured over your body. This treatment relieves pain, improves circulation, and promotes deep relaxation, helping with arthritis, muscle stiffness, and stress.

Read More

Sirodhara

Sirodhara is a soothing treatment that relieves stress, anxiety, and fatigue. It helps with insomnia, boosts mental clarity, and calms the nervous system. Perfect for rejuvenating your mind and body, it offers relief from headaches and restores balance.
